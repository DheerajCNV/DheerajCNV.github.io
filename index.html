About Me

Who I Am

Hello! I am Dheeraj, a passionate robotics engineer completing my Master's at Arizona State University. My interests lie at the intersection of Autonomous Systems, Aerial Robotics, and Machine Learning, with a strong focus on Perception, Motion Planning, and Intelligent Control. I received my Bachelor's in Mechanical Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology Tirupati in May 2023.

I love working hands-on with hardware and software and enjoy building systems that bridge theory and real-world applications. My current research at ASU involves UAV-based hyperspectral imaging for land cover and land use change (LCLUC) analysis, where I integrate deep learning models for environmental monitoring.

Over the years, I've worked on a variety of interdisciplinary projects — from developing drone swarms and remote sensing tools to simulating robotic manipulators and production systems. Whether it's programming autonomous flight paths, modelling robotic dynamics in MATLAB, or creating 3D terrain maps using ROS, I thrive at the crossroads of robotics, software, and innovation.
